# FetchPatterns - Business Case

## Executive Summary

FetchPatterns is an AI-powered document analysis platform that transforms how businesses process and understand their documents. Using advanced artificial intelligence, it extracts key insights, sentiment, and actionable intelligence from business documents in seconds—eliminating hours of manual review.

## What FetchPatterns Does

FetchPatterns analyzes business documents and provides:

### Core Analysis Features
- **Sentiment Analysis**: Determines if documents express positive, negative, or neutral emotions with confidence scores
- **Document Classification**: Automatically categorizes documents (proposals, reports, contracts, etc.)
- **Key Insights Extraction**: Identifies the most important findings and takeaways
- **Risk Flag Identification**: Highlights potential concerns and issues requiring attention
- **Keyword & Phrase Extraction**: Surfaces the most significant terms and concepts
- **Document Summarization**: Provides concise overviews of lengthy documents
- **Interactive Q&A**: Answer specific questions about document content using AI

### Visualization & Reporting
- **Word Cloud Generation**: Visual representation of key terms with customizable display (1-100 words)
- **Performance Dashboard**: Track document processing metrics and sentiment trends
- **Export Capabilities**: Download analysis as PDF reports, word clouds as PNG, or data as CSV
- **Multi-Document Analysis**: Compare and analyze patterns across multiple documents

### Document Support
- **File Formats**: PDF, DOCX, XLSX, PPTX, JPG, PNG
- **Multi-File Upload**: Process multiple documents simultaneously
- **Progress Tracking**: Real-time upload and analysis progress with visual indicators
