# FetchPatterns - Business Case

## Executive Summary

FetchPatterns is an AI-powered document analysis platform that transforms hours of manual document review into minutes of actionable insights. Using GPT-4, it provides instant sentiment analysis, risk identification, key insights extraction, and interactive Q&A across multiple business documents—reducing analysis time by 90% while improving accuracy and consistency.

## What FetchPatterns Does

**Core Capabilities:**
- **Sentiment Analysis**: Emotional tone detection with confidence scores (positive/negative/neutral)
- **Document Classification**: Automatic categorization (proposals, reports, contracts, etc.)
- **Key Insights & Risk Flags**: Critical findings and concerns extracted automatically
- **Interactive Q&A**: Ask questions about document content, get instant AI-powered answers
- **Word Cloud Visualization**: Visual representation of key terms across all documents (1-100 words)
- **Professional Reports**: One-click PDF/PNG/CSV export for stakeholder presentations

**Document Support**: PDF, DOCX, XLSX, PPTX, JPG, PNG | **Multi-file upload** with real-time progress tracking

## Who It's For

**Primary Users**: Business analysts, consultants, executives, legal teams, marketing professionals, researchers—anyone who reviews multiple documents for insights and decision-making.

**Key Industries**: Financial services (due diligence), consulting (proposal analysis), legal (contract review), healthcare (research analysis), marketing (feedback processing).
