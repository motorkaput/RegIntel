## Demo Tips & Best Practices

### Do's ✅

**Engagement**
- Ask questions: "What types of documents do you review most?"
- Relate to their pain points: "You mentioned spending hours on due diligence..."
- Use their terminology: Mirror language they use for their industry

**Demonstration**
- Let results speak: Don't over-explain, show the value
- Use real examples: Actual documents create more impact than dummy data
- Pace appropriately: Allow time for questions, don't rush

**Value Focus**
- Quantify savings: "This saved 2 hours on just this one document"
- Show business impact: "Faster decisions, better insights, competitive edge"
- Address their specific use case: Customize examples to their needs

### Don'ts ❌

**Avoid:**
- Technical jargon: Focus on business value, not technology details
- Feature dumping: Show what matters to them, not everything
- Negative comparisons: Focus on FetchPatterns benefits, not competitor weaknesses
- Over-promising: Be honest about capabilities and limitations

### Handling Objections

**Objection: "We already have document management software"**
Response: "That's great for storage and organization. FetchPatterns adds the intelligence layer—understanding what's IN those documents and extracting insights. They're complementary tools."

**Objection: "Our documents are too specialized/technical"**
Response: "The AI is trained on a broad range of technical and business content. Let's test it with one of your specialized documents right now and see how it performs."

**Objection: "We're concerned about AI accuracy"**
Response: "That's a valid concern. FetchPatterns uses GPT-4, the most advanced language model available, but we always recommend human review of AI insights—think of it as an analyst assistant, not a replacement. The time savings come from getting 90% of the analysis done instantly, leaving you to focus on the critical 10%."

**Objection: "This seems expensive"**
Response: "Let's do the math together. How many hours per month does your team spend on document review? [Calculate labor cost]. FetchPatterns typically reduces that by 90%. The ROI usually shows positive returns within the first month."

## Emergency Troubleshooting

### If Demo Breaks

**Upload Fails:**
1. Check internet connection
2. Verify file size (<10MB per file)
3. Try different file format
4. Have backup documents ready

**Slow Performance:**
1. Explain: "Processing large documents takes a moment for accuracy"
2. Use smaller demo files
3. Show completed analysis from earlier

**Authentication Issues:**
1. Have credentials ready in advance
2. Test login before demo starts
3. Have backup access method

**Feature Not Working:**
1. Move to next feature
2. Focus on what's working
3. Follow up with fix and re-demo
